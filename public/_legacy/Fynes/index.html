    About Us

    Fynes Logistics Limited was launched in 1990. The company has navigated a successful upward flight path ever since. Fynes Logistics Limited, air freight and groupage specialists has been in the Haulage Industry for twenty five years and has gained a reputation for its extensive knowledge of the industry, an excellent service and a well equipped and maintained fleet. The company has built up a thriving successful business operating throughout Ireland and Europe and providing a World Class service. We currently operate 30 vans, 10 Arctic Trucks and 5 Rigid Trucks throughout Ireland UK and Europe.
